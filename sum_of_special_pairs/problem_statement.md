## **Sum of Special Pairs**

You are given an array `A` of `N` integers.

Write a program to find the `sum` of the absolute difference between all such pairs (A[i], A[j]) such taht i < j and (j - i) is prime.

#### **Sample Input**
    input:  6
            1 2 3 5 7 12

#### **Sample Output**
    output:  45

#### **Constraints**
    1 <= N <= 1000
