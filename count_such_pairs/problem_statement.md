## **Count such pairs**

You are given an array `A` of `N` integers along with a target integer `K`.

Your task is to find out the number of pairs of integers present in the array, whose sum is equal to the target value `K`.

#### **Sample Input**
    input: 5 9
           3 0 6 2 7 

#### **Sample Output**
    output: 2

#### **Constraints**
    1 <= N <= 100
