## **Battle of Odd & Even**

You are given an array `arr` of `N` integers.

You need to print `Odd`, if the `sum of all odd numbers` present in the array is greater than `sum` of `all the even numbers` present in the array, or else print `Even`

#### **Sample Input**
    input: 4
           1 2 3 4 

#### **Sample Output**
    output: Even

#### **Constraints**
    1 <= N <= 100
    1 <= arr[i] <= 100
